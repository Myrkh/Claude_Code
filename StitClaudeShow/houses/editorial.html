03 · The trap

Do one thing, refuse another.

Every house has a characteristic failure mode. These are the traps to avoid.

Do

Use drop caps on the first paragraph of long-form content. One drop cap per page maximum.

Allow headline sizes to break the container — text that overflows feels authored.

Don't

Set all text at 14–16px because 'it's more readable.' 16–22px for body serif is correct.

Wrap a pull quote in a gray box. Let the typography do the pulling, not a container.
