02 · Motion

How this house moves.

Bouncy springs encouraged. Generous durations (340–520ms). Rewards matter in this house — animate the success moment, the checkmark, the confetti. But pick your moments: one bounce per screen.

✓--duration-moderate: 340ms  ·  hover, press

03 · The trap

Do one thing, refuse another.

Every house has a characteristic failure mode. These are the traps to avoid.

Do

Reward user actions with a celebratory bounce. The first time they complete an onboarding step.

Use big radii — 20–32px on cards. Tight radii on small elements. Concentric.

Don't

Bounce everything. Every button, every modal, every list item. It becomes noise fast.

Confuse 'playful' with 'unprofessional.' Duolingo is playful and handles language learning at scale.
