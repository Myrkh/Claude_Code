03 ·

The trap.

Playful without discipline becomes a toy store. Keep the warmth. Keep the confidence. Never mistake one for the other.

✅
Do

Reward user actions with a bounce. The first onboarding completion. The 7-day streak. The "you're done" moment.

Use big radii on cards — 24–32px for most surfaces, tight 6–8px on small elements. Concentric, always.

🚫
Don't

Bounce everything. Every button, every modal, every list item. Delight becomes noise in under ten minutes.

Confuse "playful" with "unprofessional." Duolingo handles language learning at scale. Stripe processes global payments. Don't condescend.
