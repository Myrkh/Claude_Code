02 · Motion

How this house moves.

Everything is about entrance. Scroll-driven type reveals, parallax, staggered characters. Durations are long (520–800ms) and easings are expo-out. This house is a runway show — the motion is the composition.

03 · The trap

Do one thing, refuse another.

Every house has a characteristic failure mode. These are the traps to avoid.

Do

Let display type overflow its container. It's an editorial signal, not a bug.

Use italic serif for display at 8rem+. Instrument Serif, Canela, Ogg, GT Super — the canon.

Don't

Apply Editorial-Display to a SaaS dashboard. It's a fashion show, not a workplace.

Animate body text. Reveals are for display, never for the paragraphs someone has to read.
