Plate III · the discipline

A fashion show, not a workplace.

Do

Let display type overflow.

A headline that breaks its container is signal, not mistake. Fashion magazines have done this since 1962.

Use italic serif at 8rem and above. Fraunces, Canela, Ogg, GT Super — the canon. No sans-serif for display.

Don't

Apply this house to a dashboard.

Editorial-Display is a runway show, not a workplace. Never ship it to a SaaS UI. There are seven other houses.

Animate body text. Reveals are for display. Body paragraphs are for reading — not for waiting.
